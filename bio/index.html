My name is Eva Weisert

Hi, my name is Eva Weisert, I am a multimedia artist creating through experimentation in both material and technique. I focus on obscure imagery, surrealism, and bold colors, presenting themes of change and uncertainty; I aim to remind the viewer how strange and beautiful it is to exist!

In May 2025, I graduated from Virginia Commonwealth University School of the Arts with a BFA in illustration and minor in art history. As of September 2025, I am pursuing my MA in Fine Arts at Norwich University of the Arts. You can email me with questions, commissions, or just to chat! And you can keep up with me by subscribing to my Substack blog :-)
